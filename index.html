ABOUT THE PROJECT

QUEENS COUNTY CD1

Queens Community District 1 is comprised by Long Island City, Astoria, Astoria Heights, Queensbridge, Dutch Kills, Ravenswood & Steinway

Life Expectancy

Life expectancy in CD1 ranks in the mid-to-high range of the percentile, being between 81 to 83 years.

Health Conditions

Self reports on health conditions average around 81% reporting 'Excellent' or 'Very Good'.

Population by Age

The population distribution by age in the areas of LIC and Astoria is estimated at its higher values as 41% being between 25-41 years old and 22% between 45-64.

Total estimated Population

Long Island City and Astoria count with a total population count estimated at around 201,357 people
